INTERVENTION PILLAR

PILLAR 1 Child Education Support

FPA SL will intervene in 3 areas to ensure that infants are healthy and secure, children are educated and confident and are skilled and involved in the development of their communities The child support program will support over 5,000 children between 2021 and 2025 FPASL will do the following to service pillar 1:

  • Identify school that are in dial need of educational support
  • Identify teachers and a pupil for FPASL to carry her mandate in each school
  • Conduct workshops for teachers and pupils to know their role in their projects
  • FPASL will advocate and lobby on behalf of these pupils for the support of learning materials
  • FPASL to cater for schools for the rehabilitation and furniture
  • Form human rights child protection clubs and support them. These clubs will train up children in the way they should grow.
  • FPASL staff attend CTA meetings in every term in each school to ensure the effectiveness of the project.

PILLARS 2: livelihood and Income generation for widows and vulnerable women

FPASL will identify widows and vulnerable woman and support them with micro finance, training in gardening and business management and other business venture of their choice. FPASL the beneficiaries to available market.
FPASL will organize monthly meeting with the beneficiaries for information sharing and monitoring

PILLAR 3: Child protection and gender Right

The child right and gender Bills were passed in the Sierra Leone parliament serval years ago but every little is known about them in this regard FPASL will;

  • search for and produce friendly version of these materials and educate the communities about these bill
  • public awareness raising campaigns will be launched or organized in schools and during CTA meeting
